Betty Carter's Jazz Ahead

The final performance concluding a three week long residency at the Kennedy Center in Washington DC

I was honored to be among a handful of other phenomenal musicians selected to participate in this esteemed program. My ensemble of musicians from around the world spent our time workshopping one another's compositions. We received much guidance from such greats as Jason Moran, Betty Carter, Casey Benjamin and more. My ensemble had the pleasure of opening the final concert of the residency.
